THE EXHIBITION

Words Works is the launching event and the ultimate result of a four months anti-disciplinary teaching and learning experience during the Final Synthesis Studio - C1 at the School of Design of Politecnico di Milano.
The journey started with the assignment to design a communicative experience related to a set of words about emotions or human conditions. During the exhibition the users are invited to try the communicative machines, the devices and the interactive experiences designed by the students - interpreting each word - in order to gain a higher understanding of the word itself.
